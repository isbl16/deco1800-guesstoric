Hackerspaces are about community, and we'd like to invite you to come visit ours - free. There's no restrictions, no fee and no "right" kind of person; If you're interested we'd love to show you what we've built, what where creating, and here about what you want to make. We generally open from about 5pm onwards and will have dinner at around 7. Sometimes members will have a BBQ onsite for dinner, where anyone can join. So make sure to bring some change!'. A hackerspace is a place where people with similar interests in technology, science, art, culture, and making meet. Activities are not in the slightist restricted to only computer science, but also emerge from electronics, contemporary art, photography, and any and all other pursuits worth sharing. The hackerspace offers a turnkey infrastructure to its members where inovative projects that would not come to life in a garage, can find nourishment and support.
